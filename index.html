Our Story

Yoga that holds
you gently

At Nirai Yoga, we believe every woman deserves a space where she can breathe, move, and reconnect with herself. Our mission is to support expectant mothers and women holistically — through tailored practices that honour exactly where you are in your journey.

Whether you are growing new life, recovering, or simply seeking stillness in a busy world, our classes are designed with care, compassion, and deep expertise in women's wellness.

The words we speak, the postures we hold, and the community we build together — all carry positive energy and intention.
